Improved Euler Method (Heun Method)

The Improved Euler method, also called Heun's method, is used to approximate the solution of an initial value problem:

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = f(x,y) $$$$ y(x_0) = y_0 $$

Ordinary Euler's method uses only the slope at the beginning. Heun’s method improves accuracy by averaging the initial slope and predicted slope at the end of the interval.

Algorithm

Predictor

$$ y_p = y_n + h f(x_n,y_n) $$

Corrector

$$ y_{n+1} = y_n + \frac{h}{2} [f(x_n,y_n) + f(x_{n+1},y_p)] $$
